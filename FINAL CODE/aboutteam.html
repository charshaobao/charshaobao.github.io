About Dr Tan


Dr Tan's success is built on decades of experience and a keen eye for natural-looking results based on each patient's personalised needs and skin concerns. He also travels widely to learn about the latest advancements in the field of aesthetics so that he can help his patients achieve maximum results using only the highest quality products and minimally invasive procedures.


He believes patient safety should always come first and that while every individual's path to beauty is unique, everyone deserves to look and feel their best.

Area Of Specialty


Lasers, Derma fillers, HIFU, Skin boosters, and various aesthetic procedures.

Other Interests


Dr Tan partakes in travelling and hiking during his free time and overseas trips. He enjoys making a change and difference in other's lives and is an active member of Overseas Community Immersion Programmes.

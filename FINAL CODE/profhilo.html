How does Profhilo work?

Profhilo works by allowing bio-stimulation of 4 types of collagen, elastin and proliferate adipocytes (fats) stem cells that are lost through natural ageing. It contains 100% Hyaluronic Acid (HA) without additives (no BDDE cross linking), allowing HA to spread easily with no risks of lumpiness.

Profhilo has Low and High Molecular Weight Hyaluronic Acid and this hybrid allows HA to be slowly released and last longer in the skin for bio-remodelling to occur.
High Molecular Weight Hyaluronic Acid (L-HA) Slow release into the skin for a long-lasting hydration effect, like Skinboosters. Low Molecular Weight Hyaluronic Acid (H-HA) Gives a lifting effect as the compound provides a stable hyaluronic acid structure in the dermis.

Although Profhilo’s main ingredient is hyaluronic acid, the concentration of hyaluronic acid in Profhilo is about 5 times more than your average skinbooster. It has the the highest concentration of HA (hyaluronic acid 64mg/2ml) on the market.

How is Profhilo performed?

Profhilo is injected into 5 precise Bio Aesthetic Points (BAP) on each side of the face to treat the lower parts of the face and neck. These 5 injection points are located far away from the nerves and major blood vessels which minimises risk of pain and bruising.

The dermatologist will have to apply a numbing cream on the area to be treated to lessen the pain that comes with the treatment procedure. Each injected site may have some raised bumps that can possibly lead to temporary bruising or swelling. But, these minimal side effects usually subside within the day.
